# General Ledger (GL) accounts used by the Deschutes Heights HOA (2020-2025)

## Assets (100000 Series)
- 100100 / 100150 - Operating Bank Acct: The primary checking account used to deposit dues and pay vendors. Note: In 2020, this was account 100150; it appears to have remapped to 100100 later.
- 101000 / 101050 - Reserve Bank Acct: Savings account for long-term replacements. Funded by monthly transfers from the Operating account.
- 102000 - Accounts Receivable: Money currently owed to the HOA by homeowners (dues, fines, etc.).

## Liabilities (200000 Series)
- 249994 - VIS Transfer Fees: Pass-through fees charged by the management company upon the sale of a home.
- 249996 - VIS NSF Admin Fees: Administrative fees for non-sufficient funds checks.
- 249998 - VIS Collection Letter Admin Fees: Fees charged by VIS to send collection notices.
- 249999 - VIS Compliance Admin Fees: Fees charged by VIS to process compliance violation notices.
- 250000 - Accounts Payable: Money the HOA currently owes to vendors.
- 250100 - Prepaid Income: Assessments paid by owners in advance (liability because the service period has not happened yet).

## Revenue (300000 Series)
- 300000 - Regular Assessment: The primary income source; quarterly dues paid by owners.
- 300150 - Transfer fee-Capital contribution: The $500 Conveyance Assessment collected upon home sales.
    - ⚠️ Note: this account was used to sweep Conveyance Assessment payments to Lotus House Development Corp (the Declarant) via "Payment per agreement," despite the CC&Rs restricting these funds to common area maintenance.
- 300200 - Fines and Penalties: Income from violation fines.
- 300300 - Late Fees: Income from late payment penalties.
- 300400 - Legal Fees (Passed on to Owners): Reimbursement when the HOA charges legal costs back to a specific owner.
- 300500 - Interest Earned: Bank interest on HOA accounts.
- 301100 - Miscellaneous Incomes: Rarely used; appears to handle deposit error corrections.

## Expenses (400000 Series)
### Administrative
- 400000 - Association Management Fees: Monthly flat fee paid to VIS Group.
- 400100 - Base Supply Fee - Excluding Postage: A flat monthly fee paid to VIS Group for office supplies/admin.
- 400200 - Postage: Reimbursement for mailing costs (newsletters, notices).
- 400300 - Mileage Costs: Reimbursements paid to the manager for travel to the site.
- 400400 - Audit and Tax Return: Annual CPA fees for tax filing and financial review.
- 400500 - Legal - General: General counsel fees (e.g., Burleigh Law).
- 400600 - Legal - Collections: Legal fees specifically for collecting unpaid dues.
- 400700 - Insurance: General Liability and Property insurance premiums (American Family Insurance).
- 400800 - Licenses and Permits: Secretary of State renewals and BOI reporting.
- 402100 - Meeting Expenses: Costs for venue rentals or meeting supplies.

### Utilities
- 400910 - Water: City water service.
- 400912 - Water-Irrigation: Specific account for irrigation water (City of Tumwater).
- 400920 - Electricity: Power for common areas (lights, irrigation timers).

### Maintenance & Landscaping
- 401000 - Maintenance (Common Areas): General repairs.
    - ⚠️ Note: This account contains payments to Simply Home Realty, the affiliate of the management company, sometimes for recurring identical amounts ($239.55).
- 401100 - Landscaping (Contract): The monthly flat fee for the main landscaper (Sage Landscape Maintenance; previously All You Need Landscaping).
- 401200 - Landscaping (Non-contract): Extra landscaping work (plantings, bark, repairs).
    - ⚠️ Note: Payments to Simply Home Realty also appear here in earlier years (2020).
- 401300 - Backflow/Irrigation System Maintenance: Testing and repair of the irrigation infrastructure.
- 401361 - Stormwater / Detention Pond: Maintenance of the retention ponds.

### Reserves & Other
- 401400 - Reserve Contributions: This "expense" represents the transfer of cash from Operating to Reserves (matches the deposit in account 101000).
- 401500 - Reserve Study: Fees paid to a specialist to calculate the Reserve Study (long-term budget plan).
- 401600 - Miscellaneous Expense: Small items like dog poop bags.
- 401700 - Bad Debt/Write-offs: Used when the Board waives a fine or late fee.

## Equity (600000 Series)
- 600000 - Retained Earnings: The accumulated net income/loss of the association over prior years.
- 600100 - Reserve Fund: The equity portion representing the Reserve bank account balance.
- 600200 - Prior Year Adjustments: Used for accounting corrections related to previous fiscal years.
